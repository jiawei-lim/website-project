    What is Faraday?

    Established in 1850 by one of the leading scientist at the time - Michael Faraday, Faraday was created in hopes of innovating fields of study such as engineering, medicine, science and providing cutting edge commercials products to people regardless of nationality and creed. Presently,Faraday has grown into the world's largest cooperation with over 1,000 offices worldwide, employing more than 100,000 personnel and an expanding network of orbital stations.

    How do I locate an office?

    Faraday offices and customer centers are spread across a wide range of locations. Kindly navigate the Faraday app in your phone for a more localised inquiry and result.

    How do I check the authenticity of a Faraday product?

    Every Faraday product is attached with an NFC chip that contains a unique ID. Tag the NFC chip with an NFC enabled reader to validate the product. A serial number is also imprinted on the product, input the code into the Faraday mobile app to validate the product.
